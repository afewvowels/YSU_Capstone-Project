Evaluation Procedure

The examiner instructs the patient to flex the ipslateral hip to 90 degrees and straighten the leg while the other leg remains on the table. The knee is then flexed to relieve pain.

Positive Test

Pain in lower back and glute region.

Implications

Modifications/Comments
