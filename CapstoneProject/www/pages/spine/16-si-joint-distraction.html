Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

SI Joint Distraction

Patient Position

supine.

Examiner Position

Standing next to the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

The examinar places each hand over the patient's corresponding iliac flare.

Evaluation Procedure

The examiner applies pressure down through the anterior portion of the ilium, spreading the SI joint.

Positive Test

Pain arising from the SI joint.

Implications

Sacroiliac pathology.
