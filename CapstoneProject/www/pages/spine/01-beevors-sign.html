Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Beevor's Sign

Patient Position

Supine.

Examiner Position

At the side of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

Inline with the patient's umbilicus.

Evaluation Procedure

  1. The patient's hands are placed at their side while they actively perform trunk flexion.
  2. The examinar examines the patient's umbilicus and notes for any lateral deviation while the patient's trunk is flexed.

Positive Test

Lateral, superior, or inferior deviation of the patient's umbilicus.

Implications

Thoracic motor neuron lesion/abdominal paralysis.
