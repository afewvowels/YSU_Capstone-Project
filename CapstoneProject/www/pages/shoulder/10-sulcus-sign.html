Shoulder

Sulcus Sign

Patient Position

Sitting with arm hanging at the side.

Examiner Position

Standing lateral to the involved side.

Examiner Hand Position

One hand gripping the distal forearm at the wrist and the other arm palpates the superior shoulder.

Evaluation Procedure

Inferior traction is applied to the arm.

Positive Test

A indentation appears beneath the acromion process.

Implications

Laxity of the Superior GH ligament. This is bassed off of Grades I-III:

  • Grade I = 1 CM or less
  • Grade II = 1-2 CM
  • Grade III = greater than 2 CM

Modifications/Comments

N/A

Evidence

  • Sensitivity: 30%
  • Specificity: 91%
