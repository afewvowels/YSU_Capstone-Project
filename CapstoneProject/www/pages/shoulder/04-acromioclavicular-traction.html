Acromioclavicular Traction Test

Patient Position

Sitting or standing with arm hanging naturally from the side.

Examiner Position

Standing lateral to the involved side.

Examiner Hand Position

One hand grabs the patient's humerus proximal to the elbow. The other hand palpates the AC joint.

Evaluation Procedure

Examiner applies a downward traction of the humerus.

Positive Test

Humerus or scapula move inferior to the clavicle causing step deformity, pain, or both.

Implications

Sprain of the AC or coracoclavicular ligaments.
