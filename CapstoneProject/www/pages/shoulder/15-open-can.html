Shoulder

Open Can Test

Patient Position

Sitting or standing. GH joint is abducted to 90 degrees in the scapular plane, the elbow extended, and the humerus externally rotated with forearm neutral (thumb points up).

Examiner Position

Standing facing the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

One hand is placed on the superior wrist to resist abduction in the scapular plane.

Evaluation Procedure

Evaluator applies a downward pressure.

Positive Test

Weakness and/or pain when resisting.

Implications

Supraspinatus is weak, impinged, or torn.

Modifications/Comments

Test can be performed oppositely (see empty can test).
