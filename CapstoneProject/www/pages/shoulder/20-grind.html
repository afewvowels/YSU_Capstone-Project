Shoulder

Grind Test

Patient Position

Supine with shoulder abducted to 90 degrees and elbow flexed to 90 degrees.

Examiner Position

Lateral to the patient's affect side.

Examiner Hand Position

Proximal hand on the affected anterior shoulder with the distal hand grasping the proxmal forearm.

Evaluation Procedure

Examiner applies an axial load on the humerus while internally and externally rotating the humerus.

Positive Test

Pain or replication of symptoms.

Implications

Superior Labral from Anterior to Posterior tear.
