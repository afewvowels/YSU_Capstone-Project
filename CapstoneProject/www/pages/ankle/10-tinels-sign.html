Ankle

Tinel's Sign

Patient Position

Lying supine with foot off edge of table

Examiner Position

At the foot of the patient

Examiner Hand Position

Distal hand supporting the plantar surface of the foot

Evaluation Procedure

  1. Examiner uses proximal hand's finger to tap over the medial aspect of the ankle.

Positive Test

Pain and/or tingling

Implications

Tarsal tunnel syndrome

Modifications/Comments

Positive test indicates that the posterior tibial nerve has been compromised

Evidence

Sensitivity: 58% Specificity: Inconclusive
