Thompson Test

Patient Position

Prone with the foot off the edge of the table

Examiner Position

At the side of the patient

Examiner Hand Position

One hand over the muscle belly of the calf mascultature

Evaluation Procedure

  1. Examiner squeezes the calf musculature while observing for plantar flexion of the foot

Positive Test

When the calf is squeezed, the foot does not plantar flex

Implications

Achilles tendon rupture

None
