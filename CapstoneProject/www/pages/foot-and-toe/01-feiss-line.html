Foot & Toe

Feiss Line

Patient Position

Seated with Foot on floor in neutral postion.

Examiner Position

Seated with Foot on floor in neutral postion.

Examiner Hand Position

Seated with Foot on floor in neutral postion.

Evaluation Procedure

  1. Navicular tuberosity, head of the first metatarsal, and the apex of the medial malleolus are marked with a dot.
  2. Connect the dots from the head of the first metatarsal and the medial malleolus.
  3. Note the position of the navicular tuberosity in relationship to the line connected between the head of the first metatarsal and the medial malleolus.
  4. Have the patient bear weight in a standing position.
  5. Note the postion of the navicular tuberosity of the patient's foot in the standing position.

Positive Test

N/A

Implications

Determining patient's arch type and position of navicular non-weight bearing and weight bearing.

Modifications/Comments

None
