Knee

Medial Lateral Grind Test

Back

Patient Position

Lying supine.

Examiner Position

Stands next to the involved side.

Examiner Hand Position

One hand is holding the subject's foot on the involved side and the other is placed over the joint line of the knee on the ipsilateral leg.

Evaluation Procedure

Examiner passivley flexes the subject's hip and knee, then applies a circular motion (clockwise and counterclockwise) with the tibia.

Positive Test

Pain, grinding, or clicking.

Implications

Meniscal tear.
