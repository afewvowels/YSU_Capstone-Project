Knee

Crossover Test

Patient Position

standing with the weight on the involved leg

Examiner Position

stands infront of patient

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

  1. (A) Patient steps across and infront with the uninvolved leg, rotating the torso in the direction of movement, weight bearing foot remains fixed
  2. (B) Patient steps across and behind with the uninvolved leg, rotating the torso in the direction of movement, weight bearing foot remains fixed

Positive Test

Patient reports pain, instability, or is apprehensive

Implications

  1. (A) Anterolateral Rotational Instability, instability of the lateral capsule
  2. (B) anteromedial rotational Instability, instability of the medial capsule
