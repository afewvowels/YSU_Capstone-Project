Elbow

Chair Test

Patient Position

Seated in a chair with arm rests.

Examiner Position

Standing in front of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

  1. The patient has their hands wrapped around the top of the arm rests and is seated.
  2. The patient then pushes themself up without letting go of the arm rests, and then slowly sits back down with their hands still wrapped around the top of the arm rests.

Positive Test

Pain, and/or instability in the elbow.

Implications

Sprain of the Lateral Ulnar Collateral Ligament.

Modifications/Comments

N/A

N/A
