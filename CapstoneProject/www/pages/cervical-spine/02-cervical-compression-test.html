Cervical Spine

Cervical Compression Test

Back

Patient Position

Seated.

Examiner Position

Standing behind the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

Both hands are interlocked on top of the patient's head.

Evaluation Procedure

Examiner presses their hands down, applying an axial load.

Positive Test

Patient experiences pain, in the upper cervical, upper extremity, or both.

Implications

Compression of the facet joints and narrowing of the intervertebral foramen.

Modifications/Comments

Rule out a cervical fracture or instability before performing test.

Evidence

N/A
