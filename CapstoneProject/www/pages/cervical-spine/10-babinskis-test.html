Cervical Spine

Babinski's Test

Patient Position

Supine.

Examiner Position

At the foot of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

The examiner runs their fingernail or a sharp object along the plantar aspect of the foot.

Positive Test

The patient's toes will splay out.

Implications

Upper Motor Neuron Lesion.

Modifications/Comments

This positive test in Adults is the "normal" response in babies.

Evidence

N/A
