Head & Face

Loading Test (TMJ)

Patient Position

Sitting upright in a chair.

Examiner Position

In front of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

Examiner places a cotton rolls between the subject's molars on the uninvolved side and instructs the patient to bite down forcefully.

Positive Test

Patient reports pain on the involved side.

Implications

May be reflective of an anteriorlly dislocated disk or numerous temporomandibular pathologies.
