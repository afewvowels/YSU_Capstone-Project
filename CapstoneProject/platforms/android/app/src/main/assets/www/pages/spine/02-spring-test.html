Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Spring Test (Posterior-Anterior Vertebral Joint Play Test)

Back

Patient Position

Prone.

Examiner Position

At the side of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

In contact with the patient's spinous process of the involved vertebrae.

Evaluation Procedure

The patient lies supine while the examinar places their hands directly on the patient's spine and presses anteriorly on the spinous processes.

Positive Test

The patient's verebrae does not move, moves excessively, or pain is elicited.

Implications

Facet joint hypermobility.

Modifications/Comments

N/A

Evidence

  • Sensitivity: 69%
  • Specificity: 55%
