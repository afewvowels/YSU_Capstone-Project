Wrist & Hand

Finkelstein's Test

Back

Patient Position

Seated or standing.

Examiner Position

Standing in front of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

Patient tucks thumb under fingers by making a fist.

Positive Test

Increased pain in the area of the radial styloid process and along affected musculature.

Implications

De Quervain's Syndrome (tenosynovitis of the extensor pollicis brevis and abductor pollicus longus tendons).

Modifications/Comments

Test has a high rate of false positives.

Evidence

N/A
