Cervical Spine

Roos Test

Patient Position

Standing or sitting.

Examiner Position

Standing infront of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

  1. Patient actively abducts the shoulder to 90 degrees, flexes the elbows to 90 degrees, and has their palms facing forward, making sure their elbows are slightly behind their shoulders.
  2. The patient then opens and closes their hands for 1 minute.

Positive Test

Numbness/tingling, heaviness, weakness, or any pain.

Implications

Thoracic Outlet Syndrome.
