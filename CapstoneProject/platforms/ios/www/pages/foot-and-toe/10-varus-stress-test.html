Foot & Toe

Varus Stress Test (IP Joint)

Back

Patient Position

Laying supine with legs extended.

Examiner Position

Standing lateral to the patient's affected foot.

Examiner Hand Position

The proximal hand grasps the phalange distal to the joint being tested and the other stabilizes the proximal aspect of the foot.

Evaluation Procedure

A varus stress is applied to the joint.

Positive Test

Increased gapping, compared with the same motion on the same phalange of the opposite foot or pain.

Implications

Collateral ligament sprain.
