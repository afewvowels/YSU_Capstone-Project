Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Single Leg Stance Test/Stork Test

Back

Patient Position

Standing with the body weight evenly distributed on both feet.

Examiner Position

Standing next to patient, ready to support them if they fall.

Examiner Hand Position

Ready to support patient if needed.

Evaluation Procedure

The patient lifts one leg, then places the trunk in hyperextension.

Positive Test

Pain in the lumbar spine or SI area.

Implications

Pars interarticularis pathology, vertebral pathology. SI joint irritation.
