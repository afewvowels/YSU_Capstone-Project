Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Brudzinski's Test

Back

Patient Position

supine.

Examiner Position

at the side of patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

  1. The examinar instructs the patient to flex the ipslateral hip to 90 degrees and straighten the leg while the other leg remains on the table.
  2. The patient's hands are cupped behind their head while the neck is actively flexed.
  3. The knee is then flexed to relieve pain.

Positive Test

Reaggrevation of pain symptoms in the spine.

Implications

menigeal irritation, nerve root impingment.
