Elbow

Push Up Test

Patient Position

Patient is prone on table or floor.

Examiner Position

Standing lateral to the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

The patient assumes a push up position (shoulders in line with elbows in line with wrists), then performs a full push up.

Positive Test

Pain, and/or instability in the elbow.

Implications

Sprain of the Lateral Ulnar Collateral Ligament.

Modifications/Comments

Patient may do the push up with their knees on the floor, and the push up may be done against a wall.

Evidence

N/A

Evidence Resource

Wallace PPT.
