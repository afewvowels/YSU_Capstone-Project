Head & Face

Palpation Test

Back

Patient Position

Sitting upright in chair.

Examiner Position

In front of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

Examiner places his or her 5th digits in the subject's ears.

Evaluation Procedure

The subject is instructed to repeatedly open and close the mouth while the examinar applies pressure in an anterior direction using the pads of the 5th digits.

Positive Test

Patient reports pain or discomfort during the opening and closing of the mouth when pressure is applied.

Implications

This may be a result of inflmmation to the synovium of the temporomandibular joint (TMJ).

Modifications/Comments

The subject reporting pain can be a result of any pathology to the TMJ.

N/A
