Head & Face

Chvostek's Sign (TMJ)

Back

Patient Position

The subject can either sit or stand.

Examiner Position

At the side of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

Patient's involved masseter muscle.

Evaluation Procedure

Examiner taps over the masseter muscle and parotid gland.

Positive Test

Twitching of the facial muscles especially the masseter muscle facial nerve (CN VII) pathology.

Implications

Facial nerve (CN VII) pathology
