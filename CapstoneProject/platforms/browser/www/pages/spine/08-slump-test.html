Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Slump Test

Patient Position

Sitting at the edge of the table.

Examiner Position

At the side of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

Patient actively rounds their shoulder, flexes their neck, extends the knee, and dorsiflexes the ankle.

Positive Test

Sciatic pain or sciatic pain like symptoms.

Implications

Lumbo/sacral pathology and/or disc herination.
