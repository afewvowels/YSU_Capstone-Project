Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Tension Sign

Patient Position

Supine.

Examiner Position

At the side of patient .

Examiner Hand Position

One hand graps just proximal to the heel, while the other hand grasps just proximal to the knee joint. The examiner's thumb will palpate the popliteal fossa.

Evaluation Procedure

  1. The hip is flexed to 90 degrees with the knee flexed to 90 degrees.
  2. The knee is then extended as far as possible with the examiner palpating the tibial portion of the sciatic nerve.

Positive Test

Tenderness with possible duplication of sciatic symtpoms.

Implications

Sciatic nerve irritation.
