Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Femoral Nerve Traction Test

Patient Position

Prone with a pillow under the abdomen or sidelying.

Examiner Position

At the side of patient.

Examiner Hand Position

One hand applies downward pressure on the PSIS of the affected side, while the other hand supports the knee of the affected side.

Evaluation Procedure

The examiner passively extends the hip while keeping the patient's knee flexed to 90 degrees.

Positive Test

Pain is elicited in the anterior and lateral thigh.

Implications

Nerve root impingement at the L2, L3, or L4 level.
