Thoracic & Lumbar Spine

Milgrim's test

Back

Patient Position

Supine.

Examiner Position

At the feet of the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

The patient performs a bilateral SLR to the height of 2-6 inches and is asked to hold the position for 30 seconds.

Positive Test

The patient is unable to hold the position, cannot lift the leg, or experiences pain with the test.

Implications

Intrathecal pressure causing an intervertebral disc to place pressure on a lumbar nerve root.
