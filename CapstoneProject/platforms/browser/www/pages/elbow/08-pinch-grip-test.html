Elbow

Pinch Grip Test

Patient Position

Seated or standing.

Examiner Position

Standing lateral to the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

Proximal hand is placed on the patient's affected shoulder and the distal hand's index finger and thumb are looped together inbetween the patient's index finger and thumb loop.

Evaluation Procedure

The examiner loops between the patient's loop, and attempts to break the loop of the patient.

Positive Test

Patient cannot maintain the loop they created/the patient's loop is created.

Implications

Median Nerve Compression.
