Knee

Crossover Test

Back

Patient Position

Standing with the weight on the involved leg.

Examiner Position

Stands in front of patient.

Examiner Hand Position

N/A

Evaluation Procedure

  1. Patient steps across and in front with the uninvolved leg, rotating the torso in the direction of movement, weight bearing foot remains fixed.
  2. Patient steps across and behind with the uninvolved leg, rotating the torso in the direction of movement, weight bearing foot remains fixed.

Positive Test

Patient reports pain, instability, or is apprehensive.

Implications

  1. Anterolateral Rotational Instability, instability of the lateral capsule.
  2. Anteromedial Rotational Instability, instability of the medial capsule.
