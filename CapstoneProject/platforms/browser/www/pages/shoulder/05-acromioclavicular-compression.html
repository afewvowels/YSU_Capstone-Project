Acromioclavicular Compression Test

Back

Patient Position

Sitting or standing with arm hanging naturally from the side.

Examiner Position

Standing lateral to the involved side.

Examiner Hand Position

One hand on the posterior acromion and the other on the anterior clavicle.

Evaluation Procedure

Examiner squeezes the hands together.

Positive Test

Pain at the AC joint or excursion of the clavicle over the acromion process.

Implications

Damage to the AC ligament.

Modifications/Comments

N/A

Evidence

  • Sensitivity: 79%
  • Specificity: 50%
