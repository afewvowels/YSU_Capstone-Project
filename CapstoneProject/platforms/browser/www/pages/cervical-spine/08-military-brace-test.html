Cervical Spine

Military Brace Test

Patient Position

Standing.

Examiner Position

Standing behind the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

Proximal hand on the patient's affected shoulder, and the distal hand's index and middle finger palpating the patient's radial pulse.

Evaluation Procedure

Patient actively retracts and depresses the scapula, actively hyperextends the head and neck, and the examiner passively extends and abducts the shoulder to 30 degrees.

Positive Test

Radial pulse disappears.

Implications

Subclavian artery is blocked by the costoclavicular structures of the shoulder.
