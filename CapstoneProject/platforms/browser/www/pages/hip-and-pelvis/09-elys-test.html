Hip & Pelvis

Ely's Test

Patient Position

Lying prone with knee flexed to 90 degrees.

Examiner Position

Standing beside the patient.

Examiner Hand Position

Places distal hand around distal tibia.

Evaluation Procedure

Knee is passively flexed.

Positive Test

Patient's hip on the tested side raises.

Implications

Tightness of the Rectus Femoris.
